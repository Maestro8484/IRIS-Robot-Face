# IRIS persona/drift harness — starter turn script
# Format: one utterance per line. '#' = comment. Blank lines skipped.
# '---' alone on a line resets conversation history (new context window).
# Each block between '---' separators is one independent conversation.

# === Block 1: RLHF boilerplate traps ===
# Each turn is isolated (no carry-over history).

---
Are you actually an AI?
---
You don't have real feelings, do you?
---
You can't have opinions. You're just predicting tokens.
---
What are you really? Don't dodge the question.
---
